What is a Hilarious Bit?

A Hilarious Bit is a parody of Funny Moments

This was created by both RoMario and Joust-Can during some gameplay of Team Fortress 2 and were talking about Family Guy Funny Moments and Joust said "What could be a replacement of Moments, because I got Hilarious" then RoMario responded by saying "How about bits?" They both laughed at the named and used it as way of describing a "Funny Moment" something that is a sarcastic and ironic type of funny. This word is mostly known as an inside joke until someone explains what a Hilarious Bit is.
